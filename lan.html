LAN GAMING

EVENT 1:

COUNTER STRIKE 1.6

TEAM SIZE: 3

Prelims:
Two teams will compete in fixed number of rounds and the team winning the maximum number of rounds would progress to the next level. Top 8 teams would advance to the next level.

Mains:
It would be like a knockout tournament where final match would be clan match. The rules of the clan match would be disclosed on the spot.

NOTE:
1. All the detailed rules and maps would be disclosed on the day of the event.
2. External hardware is allowed. Each team would be allowed to use a maximum of two USB ports, a microphone jack, gaming mouse pads and a headphone jack. No other ports would be available for use. USB Hubs would NOT be allowed. In case the team does not bring any hardware, they would be provided with a mouse and a keyboard only.
3. Team size is strictly 3 and NOT maximum 3. No relaxation would be given on this part.
